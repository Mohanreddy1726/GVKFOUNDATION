DIGITAL EDUCATION

More Than Technology — A Gateway To Learning

GVK Brothers Foundation provides free tablets to deserving students as part of its digital education initiative, helping bridge the gap between technology and learning. The program brings structured digital content, internet connectivity, and ongoing mentorship to learners in rural and semi-urban communities across Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

Learn more →
CAREER EMPOWERMENT

Connecting Potential, Creating New Futures

GVK Brothers Foundation empowers students and aspiring professionals through job melas, career guidance, and skill development initiatives that open doors to meaningful opportunities. Workshops on resume writing, interview preparation, and digital literacy are paired with direct employer engagement so participants leave with a plan, not just a presentation.

Learn more →
FOOD & HUNGER RELIEF

Feeding Communities, Building Hope

GVK Brothers Foundation conducts food donation initiatives to support individuals and families in need by providing nutritious meals and essential care. From weekly community kitchens to emergency ration kits during local crises, the program focuses on dignity, nutrition, and continuity of care for vulnerable households.

Learn more →
SPORTS DEVELOPMENT

Promoting Sports, Inspiring Future Champions

GVK Brothers Foundation supports aspiring athletes through sports development initiatives that provide resources, encouragement, and opportunities. From cricket kits in rural schools to fully-funded coaching for state-level competitors, the program ensures talent meets opportunity regardless of a family's means.
